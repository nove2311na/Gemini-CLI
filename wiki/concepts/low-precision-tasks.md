# Low-Precision Tasks

**Low-Precision Tasks** are activities where 90% accuracy is acceptable and errors have minimal or reversible consequences. These are the primary candidates for early-stage AI agent automation.

## Identification Framework
When evaluating tasks for automation, a "precision-based framework" helps prioritize:

- **Low-Precision (Automate First):** Data gathering, organizing information, initial drafting, formatting. Errors here are "adjust and move on."
- **High-Precision (Human-Led):** Critical financial calculations, legal compliance, high-stakes communication. Errors here have serious consequences.

## Selection Criteria
1. **Frequency:** Regularly performed tasks.
2. **Time Intensity:** Tasks that consume disproportionate time relative to strategic value.
3. **Structured Data:** Processes with clear inputs and outputs.
4. **Measurability:** Tasks with clear success metrics.

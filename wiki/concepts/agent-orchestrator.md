# Agent Orchestrator

An **Agent Orchestrator** is a professional role or skill set focused on managing, directing, and optimizing a portfolio of AI agents to achieve business or personal goals.

## Evolution of the Knowledge Worker
In 2026, the primary role of the knowledge worker shifts from being a "doer" (executing individual tasks) to an "orchestrator" (directing systems of agents).

## Key Competencies
- **Workflow Operations:** Designing and testing multi-step agent processes.
- **Agent Selection:** Building a "personal portfolio" of agents tailored to specific needs (e.g., research agents, analytics agents).
- **Quality Oversight:** Applying human judgment to verify agent outputs and align them with brand voice/strategy.
- **Data Hygiene:** Ensuring the information fed to agents is clean and relevant.

## Impact on Hiring
Agent literacy is becoming a core professional differentiator. Job postings in marketing, operations, and content creation increasingly require experience with "AI automation tools" and "agent orchestration."

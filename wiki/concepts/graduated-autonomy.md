# Graduated Autonomy

**Graduated Autonomy** is an implementation strategy where AI agents are granted increasing levels of independence as they consistently demonstrate reliability and alignment with human-defined standards.

## Implementation Path
1. **Observation/Transparency:** Humans see exactly what the agent is doing in real-time.
2. **Approval Gates:** Agents generate results but require human "sign-off" before final action (e.g., posting to social media).
3. **Periodic Sampling:** Agents act independently, with humans reviewing a subset of results for quality control.
4. **Full Autonomy:** Proven workflows where agents operate independently with only high-level monitoring of success metrics.

## Goal
To build trust and confidence while minimizing the risk of "over-automation without oversight."

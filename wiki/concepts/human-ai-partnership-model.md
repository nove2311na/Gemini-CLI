# Human-AI Partnership Model

The Human-AI Partnership Model is a strategic framework where humans and AI agents contribute complementary strengths to achieve goals more effectively than either could alone.

## Core Principle
> "Use humans for judgment and agents for execution." — Adam Biddlecombe

## Complementary Strengths

| Feature | Human Strengths | AI Agent Strengths |
|---------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Cognition** | Creativity, Intuition | Data Processing, Pattern Recognition |
| **Logic** | Ethical Judgment, Strategy | Consistent Execution, Scale |
| **Operations** | Relationship Building | Tireless Work, Multi-tasking |
| **Output** | Quality Control, Accountability | Draft Generation, Optimization |

## The Multiplier Effect
By delegating repetitive and data-intensive execution to agents, humans are "multiplied," focusing their energy on high-value creative and strategic decisions. This shifts the bottleneck from **execution capacity** to **creative direction**.

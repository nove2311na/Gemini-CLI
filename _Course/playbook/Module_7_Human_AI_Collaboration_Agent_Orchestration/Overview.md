# Module 7: Human-AI Collaboration & Agent Orchestration

## Overview
This module explores the strategic shift in 2026 from simple AI tool usage to sophisticated agent orchestration. Students will learn to design partnership models where humans provide judgment and strategic direction, while AI agents handle complex, multi-step execution.

## Lessons
1. **The Human-AI Partnership Model**: Understanding the division of labor between judgment (human) and execution (agent).
2. **Low-Precision vs. High-Precision**: How to categorize tasks for automation based on risk and error tolerance.
3. **Graduated Autonomy**: A governance framework for increasing agent independence through approval gates and sampling.
4. **Agent Orchestration**: Building and managing a portfolio of specialized AI agents.

## Key Takeaways
- Success in 2026 depends on "Agent Literacy"—the ability to direct and oversee AI systems.
- The bottleneck of productivity is shifting from **execution speed** to **decision-making quality**.
- Start small with low-risk tasks and build trust through observation.
